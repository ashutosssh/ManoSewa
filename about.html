About Mano Sewa

Mano Sewa is dedicated to providing mental health support to individuals in Nepal. Our mission is to connect people with licensed therapists and provide self-help tools to promote mental well-being. We believe in breaking the stigma surrounding mental health and providing accessible care to all.
